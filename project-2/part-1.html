    A Place

    The main character goes to a festival where he meets a singer that he idolizes. He ends up becoming friends with him and going to a festival together where he meets a girl. This text was written to be very descriptive, filled with many adjectives. In particular, lots of color descriptions are used which I can mimic with the color of the typefaces I use. Many words like “bold” are also used so that I can change the type of words accordingly. This old fairy tale reminds me of medieval times and I can use background music from that time period to accompany it. I want to make an emphasis on these descriptions on my website so that the readers know that this is a very descriptive piece of writing. I would also like to get across that this take place in medieval times with accompanying music Medieval Descriptive Old-fashioned Colorful Musical Playful Relationships Nature Beauty Chivalry Concepts: A windy road similar to the “yellow brick road” from the Wizard of Oz Words are different colors, fonts, and type to reflect what is happening in the story as well as different backgrounds and sounds
