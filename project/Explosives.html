Explosives

     Every night the park detonates hundreds of explosives known to cause death and serious injury if exploded in proximity to a person. Every year these type of explosives send ten to twenty thousand people to the emergency room in the United States alone. The pyromaniacs in the crowd love seeing these every night and often won’t go home without witnessing them.

     Every night around $50,000 is wasted on these explosives instead of using that money on improving the park’s safety.
